All your classes in one place — plus AI that helps you actually start the work.

Canvas Daily does way more than track due dates. It pulls every assignment from all your Canvas classes into one view, sorted by what's most urgent — and on any assignment you can hit “Break this down with AI” to get a research plan, an outline of the work, and ideas to beat the blank page. Setup takes about two minutes.

WHAT YOU GET

✨ AI study breakdowns — on any assignment: what's being asked, where to start researching, an outline of the work, and ideas to explore. Copy it straight into your doc and go.

📋 Every class, one view — all your assignments across every course, grouped Past due / Due today / Upcoming.

📄 Instant detail pages — read the full assignment, due date, and points without digging through Canvas.

📬 A daily email — your whole day, every class, in your inbox each morning so nothing sneaks up on you.

Get started at canvas-daily.org →
1

Sign up

Go to canvas-daily.org and create an account with your email and a password.
The email you sign up with is exactly where your daily report is sent — use the one you actually check.

2

Get your Canvas access token

This lets it read your assignments (read-only — it never submits or changes anything in Canvas):

  1. Log in to Canvas at canvas.yc.edu.
  2. Click Account (left sidebar) → Settings.
  3. Scroll to Approved Integrations → click + New Access Token.
  4. Purpose: type Canvas Daily.
  5. For Expires, click the date field and pick the latest date Canvas will let you choose — set it as far in the future as possible. (Canvas now requires an expiry date; when it eventually expires we’ll email you to make a new one.)
  6. Click Generate Token, then copy it right away — Canvas only shows it once.
3

Connect your Canvas

In Canvas Daily, go to Account → Add a Canvas connection and fill in:

Labelanything (e.g. “My classes”)
Canvas base URLhttps://canvas.yc.edu
Account typeStudent
Access tokenpaste the token from Step 2

Click Save connection — done!

That’s it. From then on every class lives in one place, an AI breakdown is one click away on any assignment, and a summary lands in your inbox each morning. You still do and submit your work in Canvas — this just helps you stay ahead and actually get started.

Your token is stored encrypted and never shown or logged again. Questions? Just reply to this email.
